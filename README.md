💰 Smart Expense Tracker
A modern and user-friendly web application for tracking personal finances, managing budgets, and monitoring expenses. The application helps users stay financially organized with interactive charts, expense reports, budget alerts, and bill reminders.

🚀 Features
     📊 Track Income and Expenses
     💰 Real-Time Balance Calculation
     🎯 Monthly Budget Management
     ⚠️ Budget Exceeded Alerts
     📈 Monthly Expense Bar Chart
     🥧 Expense Category Pie Chart
     📅 Daily Expense Report
     📆 Weekly Expense Report
     🔔 Notification Center
     🏠 Bill, Rent, and Loan Reminders
     💾 Local Storage Support
     🌙 Dark Mode Toggle
     📱 Responsive Design

🎯 How It Works
1.Set your monthly budget.
2.Add income and expense transactions.
3.View balance, income, expense, and savings summaries.
4.Analyze spending through charts and reports.
5.Add reminders for bills, rent, and loan payments.
6.Receive notifications when the budget limit is reached.

📊 Dashboard Overview
 *Balance Card – Displays current balance.
 *Income Card – Shows total income.
 *Expense Card – Shows total expenses.
 *Savings Card – Displays total savings.
 *Pending Bills Card – Shows upcoming payments.
